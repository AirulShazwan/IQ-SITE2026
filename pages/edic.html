Education Innovation Challenge (EDIC)

EDIC is a dedicated platform to showcase innovative teaching and learning practices through video presentations and demonstrations. Outstanding submissions will be awarded Gold, Silver, and Bronze medals, with certificates of participation.

EDIC Deadlines EDIC Fees

EDIC Categories

  • Micro-Credential (MC)
  • Open and Distance Learning (ODL)
  • Service Learning Malaysia – University for Society (SULAM)
  • Transformative Teaching
  • Alternative Assessment
  • Virtual, Blended, and Face-to-Face Immersive Learning Experiences
  • Empowerment of Bahasa Melayu in Teaching and Learning

Submission Requirements

  • Extended Abstract (max 4 pages): concept, objectives, methodology/implementation, outcomes, impact, sustainability
  • Video Presentation (max 3 minutes): showcase application and key highlights
  • Innovations should describe objectives, implementation, outcomes, impact, and sustainability clearly

Templates and submission links will be made available on the official website.

Awards & Recognition

Celebrate impactful innovations with recognition and certification.
